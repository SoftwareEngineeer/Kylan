Our Packages

Essentials Package

(2 application portals and up to 8 college submissions)

2 application portals: Common App, Coalition, UC, UCAS, Korea, Hong Kong, Singapore, Japan, etc.

8 college applications: Choose to submit up to 8 colleges within the 2 application portals.

For colleges/universities with their independent application site, students can apply to 1 independent school application and 7 colleges in the other 2 application portals.

Enhanced Package

(4 application portals and up to 14 college submissions)

4+ application portals: Common App, Coalition, UC, UCAS, Korea, Hong Kong, Singapore, Japan, etc.

14 college applications: Choose to submit up to 14 colleges within the 4 main application portals and other independent application sites.

Comprehensive Package

(Up to 25 college submissions on any application portal)

4+ application portals: Common App, Coalition, UC, UCAS, Korea, Hong Kong, Singapore, Japan, etc.

25 college applications: Choose to submit up to 25 colleges on any application portal and other independent application sites.
