What We Do

Kylan Consulting is a community of education consultants, admissions strategists, long-term friends, and even family members. We work with families to demystify complex admissions processes and to guide students toward personal, academic, and extracurricular excellence.

For over ten years, we have helped students gain acceptance to prestigious international boarding and high schools and colleges in the US, Canada, Hong Kong, Singapore, Australia, and beyond. Our success stories include students who have gone on to excel in their chosen fields, thanks to the guidance and support of Kylan Consulting.

Mission

We guide students toward personal, academic, and extracurricular excellence.

We demystify complex admissions processes to help students achieve their full potential, focusing on holistic development and long-term success.
