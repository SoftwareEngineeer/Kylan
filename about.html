About Kylan

Kylan Consulting is a community of education consultants, admissions strategists, long-term friends, and even family members. We work with families to demystify complex admissions processes and to guide students toward personal, academic, and extracurricular excellence. For over ten years, we have helped students gain acceptance to prestigious international boarding and high schools and colleges in the US, Canada, Hong Kong, Singapore, Australia, and beyond. Our success stories include students who have gone on to excel in their chosen fields, thanks to the guidance and support of Kylan Consulting. With founders from Seoul, Korea, Toronto, Canada, and Boston, USA, Kylan Consulting brings international experience and connections within each country, ensuring our students receive visas, housing, and ongoing mentorship.

An excellent education can be expensive, so we work with families to maximize their potential scholarship and financial aid support. Through our academic advising, admissions strategy development, application assistance, and interview preparation, students receive consistent, experienced coaching. Rest assured; we are committed to your success. We hold our students to the highest standards to help them target institutions that will give them the best opportunities in life.
