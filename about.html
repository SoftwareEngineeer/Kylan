Who We Are

Kylan Consulting is a community of education consultants, admissions strategists, long-term friends, and even family members. We work with families to demystify complex admissions processes and to guide students toward personal, academic, and extracurricular excellence.

For over ten years, we have helped students gain acceptance to prestigious international boarding and high schools and colleges in the US, Canada, Hong Kong, Singapore, Australia, and beyond. Our success stories include students who have gone on to excel in their chosen fields, thanks to the guidance and support of Kylan Consulting. With founders from Seoul, Korea, Toronto, Canada, and Boston, USA, Kylan Consulting brings international experience and connections within each country, ensuring our students receive visas, housing, and ongoing mentorship.

An excellent education can be expensive, so we work with families to maximize their potential scholarship and financial aid support. Through our academic advising, admissions strategy development, application assistance, and interview preparation, students receive consistent, experienced coaching.

Rest assured; we are committed to your success. We hold our students to the highest standards to help them target institutions that will give them the best opportunities in life.

What We Specialize In

We support families and students in gaining acceptance into prestigious international boarding and high schools and colleges in the US, Canada, Hong Kong, Singapore, Australia, and beyond.

Admissions Coaching

We partner with families throughout the application process, helping students create unique profiles that resonate with the criteria of admissions officers at top international institutions. Leveraging our experience as educators, school administrators, and admissions coaches, we deeply understand the needs of admissions officers and what differentiates an average applicant who pays higher tuition from a standout applicant whom colleges actively recruit by awarding scholarships and financial aid.

Study Abroad

From selecting the right schools and camps to simplifying the application process, we assist families in making informed decisions when applying to top international institutions. We provide guidance on various testing and visa requirements, and also delve into more nuanced topics such as whether a degree in one country will necessitate further schooling in another, and how to choose a major that will open doors to internships, work, and long-term residency.

Academic Coaching

We mold our students into competitive applicants by identifying the gaps between their abilities and the requirements of their target schools. In addition to connecting students with our network of qualified teachers, we help them to be efficient with their time and strategic with their testing. With shifting standardized testing requirements (test-optional, test-blind), various English examinations (IELTS, TOEFL, DuoLingo), entrance exam options (ACT/SAT), and curriculum options (IB/AP/A-Levels), we help students to define a clear academic path.

Personality Assessment

Our team moderates personality tests and helps students interpret the results. While an online test will measure the results from the test alone, our comprehensive analysis considers a student's personality, education and career goals, grades, passions, and extracurricular interests. Through this holistic process, we find institutions and academic pathways that align with each student's natural preferences and abilities.
